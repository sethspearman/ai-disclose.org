AI Disclosure Standard v1

A simple standard for disclosing AI use.

AI Disclose offers a lightweight, human-readable way to explain how AI contributed to a piece of content.

Disclosure Levels

No AI Used

No AI tools were used in creating this content.

Proofread by AI

AI was used only for grammar, spelling, or minor corrections.

Edited by AI

I wrote this content. AI helped improve clarity, tone, or flow.

Co-written with AI

This content was developed collaboratively between me and AI.

Authored with Guidance

I provided key points or direction. AI generated the draft.

AI Generated

This content was generated primarily by AI from a prompt.

AI Disclosure: No AI used
